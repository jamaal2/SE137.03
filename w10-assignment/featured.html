Pay Calculator Form

This project is a Pay Calculator Form I made in my HTML Class. It allows users to calculate their monthly pay based on their hourly rate and hours worked.

Some features of the Pay Calculator Form:

  1. Input fields for hourly rate and hours worked
  2. Calculate button to compute the monthly pay
  3. Result display for the calculated pay

Feel free to try it out and see how it works!
